Transcendent Survival

Feeling lost? Are you in a rut? Maybe you feel like you are just another face is the crowed? Want to stand out? Better yourself? Well, Transcendentalism may be able to help. We provide a one month survival retreat where you detach from society, no electronics, no communication with society, live off the land, and better your mind. With the teachings of Transcendentalism, this journey can help guide you to self actualization and self reliance. It causes you to provide all the necessities of life for yourself allowing you to stop relying on other people and entities. This retreat also lets you to get away from all the stresses of life and the materialistic world and simply allows you to be one with the world and nature.


While on your trip you might consider doing the following.

  • Do not always take the same path when going to or from places.
  • Do some soul searching.
  • Asses your life and decide if you are really happy in life.
