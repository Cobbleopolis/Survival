Transcendent Survival

Feeling lost? Are you in a rut? Maybe you feel like you are just another face is the crowd? Want to stand out? Better yourself? Well, Transcendentalism may be able to help. We provide a one-month survival retreat where you detach from society, no electronics, no communication with society, live off the land and better your mind. With the teachings of Transcendentalism, this journey can help guide you to Self-actualization and self-reliance. It causes you to provide all the necessities of life for yourself allowing you to stop relying on other people and entities. This retreat also lets you get away from all the stresses of life and the materialistic world and simply allows you to be one with the world and nature.


During this journey, you will break away from society. Secede from its tyrannous rule over its individual, become your own person. No longer conform to their standards or rules. Become a true individual. Find your own path, discover your true purpose. Realize how society is only taking away your individuality and liberty.The isolation allows you to redefine yourself without influence from any person or entity.


Helpful tips to make your journey more enjoyable is that you may want to bring a journal with you to keep track of the days and document your journey. another do not always take the same path when going to or from places or change the order of what you do each day, nothing will change, and, therefore, no progress will be made if you do the same things over and over again.

Survival Information

The recommended location for this type of retreat is Cook Island, Maine or some similar site. A map of the island can be found below as well as some other information about the location such as natural resources and average weather. Also there is a list of supplies below, this is only a suggested list and items can be added or removed as needed although a hard $500 limit must be set to prevent you from carrying too many materialistic items with you.


Although removing oneself from society for a large period of time is not easy it can be done. You do have to plan for your eventual return to society, like communicating to your employer that do intend to eventually, return to work. Also, inform your family that you will embark on this trip and will not be able to be contacted during this time.


Skills that may be useful to learn:

  • Some form of hunting
  • How to properly use a gillnet (this is also know as net fishing)
  • How to properly purify water
  • First aid

Things to note:

  • The temperatures are averages taken over the year of 2014 and are subject to change.
  • The information provided is only valid for Cook Island, Maine
  • You are responsible for bringing any extra materials you deem necessary.
  • You are responsible for providing your own transportation to the location you have chosen as well as whatever you use to cary your supplies but it must be able to contain all of your supplies.
